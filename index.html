BetaHiker

This website is about hiking in the Pyrenees, and hiking the GR10 trail in particular.

We want to help both beginner and experienced hikers who are interested in hiking the GR10. This website is intended to be a broad information source on hiking the GR10. You can find information about hiking a small section of the trail for a short vacation and about hiking the entire trail. There is a lot of practical information, such as logistics, food on the GR10, and a hiking GR10 checklist. If you find any of the information on this website unclear, or if you have any other questions about hiking the GR10, please don't hesitate to contact us.
